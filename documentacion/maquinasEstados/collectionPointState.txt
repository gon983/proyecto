Pending → The delivery point has been registered but has not yet been activated or assigned.
Active → The delivery point is available to receive or deliver packages/books.
On hold → Temporarily unavailable but may be reactivated soon.
Under maintenance → Cannot be used due to repairs or updates.
Saturated → Cannot receive more deliveries due to maximum capacity.
Under review → Its status or compliance with regulations is being verified.
Deactivated → Has been manually or automatically disabled.
Deleted → No longer exists in the system or has been marked for deletion.
